My Hobbies

Being Silly

If you haven't noticed by this point, I may be worried about you. One of my favorite amusements in life is to make the mundane more joyous. I don't always do a spectacular job, but I do put my all into it. I believe life will always be more worth living if we all find ways to smile and laugh more.

Visual Entertainment

I've spent copious amounts of time indulging in the joy of Film, Television, and Anime. I enjoy exploring new ones and revisiting old ones. It has proven, throughout the years, to be fun alone or with friends.

Here are a few I love with brief descriptions.

There's Something About Mary
A romantic comedy with a wonderful premise, fantastic comedic bits, and great chemistry between the main characters.
Chuck
A hilarious yet cheesey spy show that also has some amazing chemistry between it's entire main cast.
One Piece
Currently my favorite anime with an amazing ensemble of main characters, gut busting comedy, and some of the most extensive and incredible world building I've seen to date.
